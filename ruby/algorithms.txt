** Directions for the robot to get to 10th floor of a building **

1. approach elevator
2. search signs near elevator to find out what floor you are on
3. locate up or down buttons near elevator

4. if current floor > 10,
    + press down button
5. else
    + press up button
    
6. when elevator door opens, enter elevator
7. face elevator buttons
8. locate button with the number "10"
9. press and release the button
10. when elevator doors open, scan the floor level elevator is currently at

11. if current floor of elevator does not equal 10,
     + stay on elevator
12. else
     + exit elevator
